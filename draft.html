Draft Strategy

Optimized for Chabels scoring — defense and efficiency win championships.

🛡️ Prioritize Big Men

Blocks = 4 pts. A center averaging 2+ BPG = 8+ fantasy pts from blocks alone.

🎯 Target Efficiency

FGM = +2, FGA = -1. A 50% shooter = +0.5/attempt. A 40% shooter = -0.2. Volume + bad % = disaster.

🏀 Double-Double Hunters

+5 DD bonus, +10 TD. Target stat-stuffers who fill every category.

🤚 Steals Are Gold

4 pts per steal. Wing defenders averaging 1.5+ SPG are hidden gems.

🎯 Playmakers Get Paid

Assists = 2 pts (2x standard). Pass-first guards and playmaking forwards carry extra weight.

⚠️ Beware Volume Scorers

Check TS% before PPG. High-usage + low efficiency = Chabels trap.

Christian/Mitch

Biggest need: young wing runway. SGA, Brunson, Harden, Curry, AD, and Bam are loaded for now, but the roster needs future-proof forwards before the veteran guard core ages out.

Best rookie profile: SF/PF with steals, blocks, and enough scoring to become more than a specialist.

Schommer

Biggest need: guard creation. Jokic, KAT, Clingan, Ingram, Hart, Vassell, and Kuminga give size and wing production; the roster is lightest on true assist-driving guards.

Best rookie profile: PG/SG with assists, steals, and a path to starter minutes.

CJ

Biggest need: durable frontcourt youth. Embiid, Zion, Jimmy, Gafford, Ware, and Queta create big-week upside, but injuries and age make a young big or two-way forward the cleanest need.

Best rookie profile: rim protector or efficient forward who rebounds and blocks shots.

Noah

Best rookie profile: PF/C with double-double equity and real defensive stats.

Peter

Biggest need: young two-way wings. Giannis, Sengun, Jaylen Brown, Ja, Jamal Murray, Brandon Miller, Porzingis, and Ayton are strong; the next layer should be athletic SF/PF depth with stocks.

Best rookie profile: wing/forward who can defend, rebound, and add efficient secondary scoring.

Kaleb

Biggest need: shooting and guard/wing depth. Luka, Cade, Paolo, Mobley, Gobert, Lively, Mark Williams, and Dylan Harper give elite top-end talent; the roster needs perimeter efficiency around the stars.

Best rookie profile: SG/SF who hits threes, gets steals, and does not need high usage.

Logan

Biggest need: young center depth and post-KD insulation. Maxey, Ant, LaMelo, Scottie Barnes, Jalen Williams, KD, Hartenstein, and Vucevic can contend, but the future frontcourt needs youth.

Best rookie profile: late-round big with blocks/rebounds or a stash guard with assists.
