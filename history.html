📖 League Lore

The Parity Era

Three seasons, three different champions. No repeat winners. No dynasty... yet. This league is wide open, and whoever wins in 2026-27 either joins the one-ring club or becomes the first to go back-to-back.

The Peter Ascent

Peter Lundquist's squad has been the most consistent team in league history — finishing 4th, 4th, and 3rd in three seasons with an improving record each year (22→25→30 wins). A ring is the only thing missing from the résumé.

The 37-3 Curse

Kaleb posted the most dominant regular season in Chabels history in 2025-26 — 37-3 with the most points scored ever. And still didn't win it all. Logan proved that regular season dominance means nothing come playoff time.

The Kwame Brown Award

The league's loser trophy goes to the bottom finisher each season. A mark of shame — and motivation. Past recipients know who they are.
