How It Works

Each team's roster is scored against Hashtag Basketball's Dynasty Rankings — a 50/50 blend of each team's Top-15 average rank (pure talent) and age-adjusted roster depth. No draft picks, no historical record. Player values shift by age based on the time window — young studs matter more in long-term views, while veterans hold value for next season.
