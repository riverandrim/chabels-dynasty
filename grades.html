2026 DRAFT GRADES

Every rookie class graded on five equally-weighted factors. Ownership pulled live from the actual draft — trades included.

How These Grades Work

VALUEPlayer ranking vs. the pick used. A top-15 guy in round 3 is a steal; reaching is a ding.
SCORING FITChabels scoring: STL/BLK ×4, AST ×2, FGM +2/FGA −1, DD +5. Rewards stocks & efficiency.
ROSTER FITDid the class fill the team's actual holes? Computed from each owner's live roster needs.
TALENTRaw asset quality — average prospect rank of the class. Elite players lift this.
YOUTHDynasty timeline. Younger classes with runway score higher; aging vets lower.

Each factor is 20% of the overall grade. Ranks & projections from Dizzle Dynasty 6.0 (Points tab); roster needs from Hashtag Points dynasty ranks. Veterans outside the rookie pool graded neutral. Curved across the league.
